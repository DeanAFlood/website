FINAL FANTASY XIV

ENDWALKER

Final Fantasy XIV is a high fantasy massively multiplayer only role-playing game developed by Square Enix for personal computers and Playstation. Initially released in September 2010 after a critical and commercial failure was remade from almost the ground up by Naoki Yoshida and re-released in August 2013 to a much more receptive audience. The game is now on its 4th expansion and has went on to become the highest grossing Final Fantasy game of all time even beating out Final Fantasy VII
