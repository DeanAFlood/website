The first of the starter cities, the sea fairing city of Limsa Lominsa, home to the marauder, rogue and arcanist classes.
The second of the starter cities, the Dessert city of Ul'Dah, home to the Pugilist, Black Mage and Gladiator classes'
The final of the starter cities, the forest based city of Gridania is home to the Lancer, Conjurer and the Archer classes
The noble elven city of Ishgard opens its gates upon the start of the Heavensward expansion and allows players to acquire the Dark Knight, Machinist and Astrologian classes for their characters
The Far East port city Kugane is where players spend their down time during Stormblood while also being given many jumping puzzles as the city scape itself is very easy to jump around on.
The other worldly Crystarium is host to the mysterious and imposing Crystal tower and is where players spend their time in the ShadowBringers expansion
The Scholar filled city of Old Sharlayan is host to many secrets that the Forum hold which the players must find out about in the EndWalker expansion
